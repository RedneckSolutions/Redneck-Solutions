Our story

Redneck Solutions was created for business owners who need straight answers, useful strategy, and problem-solving that works outside of a boardroom.

We know most business problems are not neat, pretty, or perfectly labeled. Sometimes the issue is cash flow. Sometimes it is marketing. Sometimes it is a messy process, bad pricing, wasted labor, poor systems, inventory confusion, or the simple fact that nobody has stepped back far enough to see the whole picture.

That is where we come in. Tyler brings hands-on entrepreneurial experience from starting, running, and growing real businesses. Laurel brings experience in web development, marketing, software development, HR, payroll, accounting, and finances. Together, we combine practical field experience with digital, operational, and financial strategy to help businesses clean up the chaos and move forward.
