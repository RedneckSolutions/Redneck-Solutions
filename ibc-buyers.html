Chemical IBC Tote Uses

DIY Utility Builds

People use chemical totes for all kinds of redneck-engineered brilliance: storage builds, water trailers, utility tanks, and shop projects.

Use Common Sense

Chemical totes are not for drinking water, food storage, or animal drinking water unless properly verified, cleaned, and confirmed safe for that exact use.

Important safety note

Pick the right tote. Don’t play mystery-liquid roulette.

Food-grade and chemical totes are not the same thing. Chemical totes should not be used for drinking water, food storage, or animal drinking water unless they have been properly verified, cleaned, and confirmed safe for that specific use.

Buyers are responsible for making sure the tote they buy is right for the job. If you’re not sure which type you need, email us and tell us what you’re trying to do. We’ll help you think it through.
