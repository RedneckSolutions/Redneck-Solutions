Safety note

Match the tote to the job.

Chemical totes and food-grade totes are not the same thing. Used chemical totes should not be used for drinking water, food storage, or animal drinking water unless they have been properly verified, cleaned, and confirmed safe for that specific use.

Buyers are responsible for making sure the tote is appropriate for their intended use. If you are unsure, ask before buying.
