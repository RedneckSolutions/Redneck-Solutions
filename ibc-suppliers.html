What we need to know before we haul.

To evaluate pickup, we need to know how many totes you have, where they are located, whether they are chemical or food-grade, what was previously stored in them, whether they are empty, and whether they are easy to access for loading.

We may not be able to accept every tote. Leaking, unsafe, restricted, unknown, heavily contaminated, or inaccessible totes may not qualify for pickup.
