Important Note

Used IBC totes are not all the same. Previous contents, condition, food-grade status, and intended use matter.

Food-grade availability may vary. Chemical totes should not be used for drinking water, food storage, or animal drinking water unless they have been properly verified, cleaned, and confirmed safe for that specific use.

Buyers are responsible for making sure the tote they purchase is appropriate for their intended use. Companies requesting pickup should provide details about previous contents, quantity, tote condition, and location.
