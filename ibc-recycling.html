Important note

Chemical and food-grade totes are not the same thing.

Used IBC totes are sold based on current availability and known information. Food-grade availability may vary. Chemical totes should not be used for drinking water, food storage, or animal drinking water unless they have been properly verified, cleaned, and confirmed safe for that specific use.

Buyers are responsible for making sure the tote they purchase is appropriate for their intended use. When in doubt, ask before buying. Better safe than sorry, because mystery-liquid roulette is not a business plan.
